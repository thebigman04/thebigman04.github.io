Welcome to Troop 336!

Troop 336 has been in existence since June 1954. Over the years we have produced over 60 Eagle Scouts. Some of them have gone on to the United States Military Academy at West Point. We are an "outings" oriented Troop, having an outdoor event planned nearly every month of the year. Troop 336 is committed to engaging boys into becoming young men through the Scouting program.

The mission of the Boy Scouts of America is to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law. Any boy between the ages of 11 and 18 is welcome to join us. Please call Scoutmaster Steve Maroney if you are intrested in joining Troop 336. It will be the "Best Decision of Your Life!"
